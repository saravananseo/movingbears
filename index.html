FAQs

Common Questions

Why don't I see the mover's name before I book?+
We intentionally keep mover identities anonymous until after payment to protect both you and our network. This prevents movers from contacting you directly off-platform, ensures pricing integrity, and lets you choose based purely on ratings and guaranteed price — not brand familiarity or sales tactics. You see real reviews, ratings, credentials, and guaranteed pricing. You just don't see the company name until you're secured.
Who do I pay and how does the money work?+
You pay MovingBears directly — not the moving company. We act as the merchant of record and hold your payment in escrow. Once your move is completed and confirmed, we release the mover's pre-negotiated fee to them and retain our margin. This protects you completely — the mover is motivated to perform because they don't get paid until you say so.
What if something goes wrong with my move?+
Because you contracted with MovingBears (not the mover directly), we are fully accountable. Contact our support team — not the mover — and we will handle the resolution immediately. This includes arranging replacements, processing damage claims, negotiating compensation, and ensuring every issue is resolved before we release any payment to the mover.
How do you select which movers to present to me?+
Our team (and over time, our pricing algorithm) selects movers based on route coverage, current availability, specialty match (e.g., piano movers for piano jobs), performance rating, and capacity. We negotiate their rate behind the scenes and only present you with movers we've confirmed can take your job at or above our quality standard. You get the best fit — not just the lowest bidder.
Is MovingBears free to use?+
Getting a quote is completely free with no obligation. When you book, a service fee (included in your flat-rate price) covers our escrow system, guarantee protection, and 24/7 customer support. There are no hidden fees — the price you see is the price you pay, guaranteed.
How are movers vetted to become MovingBears Certified?+
Every mover must pass: DOT license and authority verification, minimum $1M cargo and liability insurance validation, owner and crew background checks through our SaferWatch partnership, and an initial performance review period. Movers must maintain a minimum 4.5-star rating across completed jobs to remain certified. Underperforming movers are removed — protecting the quality of every job in our network.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does MovingBears work?+
MovingBears is an online moving marketplace that connects customers with certified, insured moving companies who compete for your business. Unlike calling a single company, you post your move details once and receive competing bids from multiple movers — then choose the best price, rating, and timeline. It's always free to post, and there's no obligation to book.
Why do moving prices vary on MovingBears?+
Many factors affect pricing: distance, home size, time of year, access difficulty (stairs, long carries), special items (pianos, safes), and mover availability. Since multiple certified movers bid on your job, you naturally get competitive market pricing — customers typically save 20–30% compared to calling a single company directly.
How are movers vetted and certified?+
Every mover on MovingBears goes through a rigorous certification process: license and DOT number verification, insurance validation (minimum $1M liability), background checks on owners and crew, and ongoing performance monitoring through our SaferWatch partnership. Movers must maintain a minimum 4.5-star rating to remain on the platform.
What is the MovingBears Guarantee?+
The MovingBears Guarantee means we've got your back if something goes wrong. If a mover fails to show up, causes damage, or doesn't complete the job, our team intervenes immediately — negotiating on your behalf, arranging a replacement mover, and providing compensation. Your payment is held in escrow until you confirm your move is complete.
How does payment work?+
Payments are held securely in escrow through MovingBears. When you book a mover, your funds are protected and only released to the mover after you confirm your move is complete. This means movers are motivated to do excellent work, and you're never at risk of paying for a job that wasn't done right.
How much does MovingBears charge?+
Posting your move is completely free — no credit card required. When you book a mover, a small service fee (3–5% of the move cost) is added to your total. This fee covers our escrow payment system, guarantee protection, and 24/7 customer support. Movers also pay a platform commission of 8–12% per completed job — this is how we sustain the marketplace.
How quickly will I receive bids?+
Most customers receive their first bid within 1–2 hours of posting, with the majority receiving 3–5 competing bids within 4 hours during business hours. For complex or specialty moves, it may take up to 24 hours for all qualified movers to review and respond. You'll receive email and SMS notifications as each bid comes in.
